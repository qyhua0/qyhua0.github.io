About Me

About Me

My name is Huang Zhenhua. I am a software developer and science fiction writer.

I write about artificial intelligence, predictive systems, and future society. Through both code and storytelling, I explore how technology shapes human destiny and what lies ahead in our increasingly digital world.

With over 17 years of experience in software development, I specialize in Java full-stack development, AI applications, and building scalable systems. My technical background informs my fiction, creating authentic narratives about technology's impact on humanity.

I believe in the power of both practical engineering and imaginative storytelling to shape the future. Whether building enterprise systems or crafting speculative fiction, I aim to create work that is meaningful, impactful, and forward-thinking.
